Butter Chicken

Time: 45 Minutes Servings: 4 Difficulty: Medium Calories: 420 kcal
Prep Time
20 min
Cook Time
25 min
Servings
4 people
Difficulty
Medium
Cuisine
Indian

Step-by-Step Instructions

01

Marinate the Chicken

In a large bowl, combine the chicken pieces with yogurt, lemon juice, 1 tsp cumin, 1 tsp garam masala, turmeric, and chilli powder. Mix well, cover, and marinate for at least 1 hour (overnight in the fridge is best).

02

Grill or Pan-Sear the Chicken

Heat a grill pan or heavy skillet over high heat with a little oil. Cook the marinated chicken pieces for 3–4 minutes per side until slightly charred and cooked through. Set aside.

03

Make the Makhani Sauce

In a saucepan, melt butter over medium heat. Add chopped onion, garlic, and ginger. Sauté for 5–6 minutes until golden. Add remaining spices and cook for 1 minute. Stir in the crushed tomatoes and simmer for 15 minutes until thickened.

04

Blend the Sauce

Allow the sauce to cool slightly, then blend it to a smooth purée using a blender or hand blender. Pass through a sieve for an extra silky texture. Return to the pan.

05

Combine & Finish

Add the grilled chicken to the blended sauce. Pour in the heavy cream and stir gently. Simmer on low heat for 8–10 minutes. Taste and adjust salt. Finish with a swirl of butter and a sprinkle of garam masala.

06

Serve

Serve hot garnished with fresh coriander and a small knob of butter on top. Accompany with basmati rice or warm naan bread. Enjoy!

💡 Chef's Tip: For extra depth of flavour, use fire-roasted tomatoes instead of regular crushed tomatoes. Kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves) added in the last 2 minutes is the secret ingredient of most restaurant-style butter chicken.

Ingredients

  • 700g boneless chicken, cut into pieces
  • ½ cup plain yogurt
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp chilli powder
  • 2 tsp garam masala (divided)
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 3 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 5 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, grated
  • 400g crushed tomatoes
  • 200ml heavy cream
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp kasuri methi (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander to garnish
