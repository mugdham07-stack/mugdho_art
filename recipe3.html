Paneer Tikka

Time: 35 Minutes Servings: 4 Difficulty: Easy Calories: 280 kcal
Prep Time
15 min
Cook Time
20 min
Servings
4 skewers
Difficulty
Easy
Cuisine
South Asian

Step-by-Step Instructions

01

Prepare the Paneer

Cut paneer into 1-inch cubes and keep aside. Soak wooden skewers in water for 30 minutes if using them. Cut vegetables into similar-sized pieces for even cooking.

02

Make the Marinade

In a bowl, combine yogurt, red chili powder, turmeric powder, cumin powder, ginger-garlic paste, lemon juice, and salt. The yogurt should be thick and act as a good coating for the paneer.

03

Marinate the Ingredients

Coat paneer cubes and vegetables thoroughly with the marinade. Let them sit for at least 30 minutes in the refrigerator for better flavor absorption. This step can be done a few hours in advance.

04

Thread onto Skewers

Alternate paneer cubes with vegetables on skewers. Leave a little space between pieces for even cooking. Brush with oil to prevent sticking.

05

Grill or Broil

Preheat grill or oven broiler to 200°C. Grill skewers for 12-15 minutes, turning occasionally, until paneer is lightly charred and vegetables are cooked through. Avoid overcooking or paneer will become rubbery.

💡 Chef's Tip: For best results, use fresh paneer and firm vegetables. Don't over-marinate as the paneer can absorb too much moisture. Charred edges add wonderful smoky flavor. Serve immediately after grilling with mint chutney.

Ingredients

  • 500g paneer, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 1 cup thick yogurt
  • 2 tbsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 3 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 3 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 bell pepper, cut into pieces
  • 1 onion, cut into chunks
  • 8-10 mushrooms
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • Fresh coriander for garnish
  • Mint chutney for serving
